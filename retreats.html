The Book

Retreats

Room to breathe. Time to come undone well.

Formation retreats for women’s ministries and leadership teams, built from the book’s three movements and paced for actual rest.

Why a retreat

Some things can’t happen in a ninety-minute session. The women who need this book most are the ones who never stop moving, and a retreat gives them what a keynote can’t: unhurried time with Scripture, honest conversation, and space where nothing is required of them. Jodi builds each retreat with your team so it fits your women, your venue, and your weekend.

The shape of it

Every retreat moves through the book’s three parts. What Binds Us makes it safe to tell the truth about what binds, the fear and shame and unbelief that keep a capable woman running. The Unraveling walks into the wilderness words, where God meets his people in the unmade places and does not hurry them through. Walking Forward turns toward what he is forming now, and the women leave with practices they can carry home, along with more freedom than they arrived with.
