What Jodi Speaks On

Formation for women

For the room full of women holding everything together. What happens when faithfulness itself becomes the thing we serve, and how God kindly takes it apart. Told through Jodi’s own story, with Scripture carrying the weight.

Best for: women’s events, retreats

From the Hebrew words

A Hebrew word anchors every chapter, from tohu v’vohu to tikkun olam, and each one opens up something about how God remakes a person. Rich teaching with plenty of room for the heart, shaped to the time your gathering can give it.

Best for: Bible studies, conferences

The gifts you carry

The gifts God placed in you are often the ones you dismiss as nothing special. This talk brings the book’s discovery questions into the room. Jodi’s own answer led to Northeast Africa. Yours will look like your own life.

Best for: conferences, mission weekends
